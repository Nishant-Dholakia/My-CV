Youth Guider


Why Today's Youth Need This?


Today, our youth face depression and addiction to drugs due to influences from social media. Every day, numerous suicides occur worldwide. Some reports suggest that a major pandemic of suicides may emerge in the next 10 years. To address this, I have developed this application.


How It Works?



It tracks youth's activities 24/7. Based on this data, it provides guidance, and these reports are sent to their parents. Through this, they understand the value of life, learn, and experience spirituality.

Animal Tracker


This project aims to assist animals roaming on streets. We will develop a device capable of tracking animals' current locations and monitoring their health details, including pregnancy-related health issues. We intend to provide this information to government agencies and highway authorities to prevent road accidents caused by animals and stop the harassment of animals. We plan to collaborate with governmental bodies and NGOs for this initiative.
